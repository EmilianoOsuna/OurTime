### What's Working
1. **Editorial Aesthetic:** The use of `Newsreader` paired with `Hanken Grotesk` and the warm "paper" background creates a premium, nostalgic feel.
2. **Native Feel:** Outstanding use of `framer-motion` for transitions, gesture-driven bottom sheets, and native back-button integration.
3. **Contextual Theming:** Dynamically shifting accent colors based on the category (pareja, amigos, familia) via CSS overrides is a brilliant immersive touch.

### Priority Issues
- **[P1] Design System Drift in Profile/PlanDetail**: The use of generic Material UI blues (`#1976D2`) and stark whites (`#fff`) breaks the warm, immersive editorial aesthetic.
  - **Why it matters:** It ruins the carefully crafted "Refugio Cálido" feel, making those screens feel like a generic tech app.
  - **Fix:** Replace hardcoded colors with tokens (e.g., `var(--ink)`, `var(--blue)`, `var(--paper)`, `var(--card)`).
  - **Suggested command:** `/impeccable polish`
- **[P2] Scalability of the Timeline**: `Dashboard.tsx` maps over all `past` plans. For long-term users, rendering hundreds of plans simultaneously will degrade DOM performance.
  - **Why it matters:** The app will become slow and unresponsive as the relationship grows (which is exactly when the app should shine most).
  - **Fix:** Implement pagination, virtualization, or a "Ver años anteriores" grouped view.
  - **Suggested command:** `/impeccable optimize`
- **[P3] Contrast on Glass Nav**: `.ot-glass-nav` uses `rgba(255,252,247,0.88)`. Depending on scrolling content, inactive icons (`var(--ink-faint)`) might fail contrast accessibility.
  - **Why it matters:** Hard to read or navigate for users with visual impairments or in bright sunlight.
  - **Fix:** Increase the blur, or slightly darken the inactive icon state.
  - **Suggested command:** `/impeccable audit`
- **[P3] Tab State Retention**: `AppShell` conditionally renders tabs inside a single `motion.div`. Switching tabs unmounts/remounts components, losing scroll position.
  - **Why it matters:** Breaks the "native app" illusion. Users expect to return to a tab exactly where they left off.
  - **Fix:** Mount all tabs but visually hide the inactive ones, or use a router that preserves state.
  - **Suggested command:** `/impeccable harden`
